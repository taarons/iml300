Website Critique

Typography

1. Good Typography

I like that the New Yorker balances a minimalistic aesthetic with a moderate amount of style. It's visually appealing and user interface friendly.

2. Bad Typography

The website doesn't lack interface as it easily presents users with contact information and opportunities to donate. However, the overlaying of images with inconsistent resolutions and the hyper colorized page makes for a rather distracting and unpleasant look.
